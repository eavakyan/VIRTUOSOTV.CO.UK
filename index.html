About VIRTUOSO TV

Bringing classical music, opera, and theater into your home

VIRTUOSO TV is the premier performing arts television channel, dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich heritage of classical music, opera, and theater.

We feature on-demand performances from the world's most prestigious concert halls, opera houses, and theaters — from intimate recitals and grand opera productions to captivating stage plays, ballet performances, and in-depth documentaries exploring the lives of great composers, performers, and playwrights.

Our mission is to make the performing arts accessible to everyone, from seasoned aficionados to curious newcomers discovering classical music, opera, and theater for the first time.

Our Programming

Discover a world of classical music, opera, and theater excellence

Symphonic Orchestras

Grand symphonic performances from the Berlin Philharmonic, Vienna Philharmonic, and leading orchestras worldwide.

Grand Opera

Complete productions from the world's most prestigious opera houses, from timeless classics to modern premieres.

Ballet & Dance

Breathtaking performances from renowned ballet and dance companies, set to classical masterpieces on the world's great stages.

Chamber Music

Refined ensemble performances — from string quartets and piano trios to wind quintets — in intimate settings that reveal every nuance.

Solo Recitals

Virtuoso performances from pianists, violinists, cellists, and vocalists — showcasing artistry at its most personal and powerful.

Theater & Stage

From Shakespeare to bold new works — drama, musicals, and live productions from the world's leading theater companies and performers.

Conservatory Showcases

Standout performances from the world's leading music conservatories and drama schools — where the next generation of great artists is taking shape.

Masterclasses

World-renowned musicians and performers share their craft — technique, interpretation, and artistry in intimate teaching sessions for aspiring professionals.

School & Academy Productions

Celebrating excellence in performing arts education — concerts, recitals, and stage productions from schools and academies nurturing young talent.

Young Artist Spotlights

Profiles and performances from exceptional emerging talent — the students and early-career artists making their mark on the classical and performing arts scene.

Competitions & Festivals

From first rounds to triumphant finals — coverage of the world's most prestigious music competitions and performing arts festivals.

Exclusive Interviews

Intimate conversations with legendary conductors, soloists, directors, and rising stars of the performing arts world.
