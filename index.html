About VIRTUOSO TV

Bringing classical music into your home since 2024

VIRTUOSO TV is the United Kingdom's premier classical music television channel, dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich heritage of classical music.

We broadcast 24 hours a day, featuring live performances from the world's most prestigious concert halls, intimate recitals, opera productions, ballet performances, and in-depth documentaries exploring the lives of great composers and musicians.

Our mission is to make classical music accessible to everyone, from seasoned aficionados to curious newcomers discovering the genre for the first time.
